Minicraft for PSP

Playstation Portable Port of Notch's game for Ludum Dare 22. It is top-down Zelda-like Minecraft little brother. Chop trees by hands, craft weapons and fight monsters. Make your way to the deepest dungeon and get back to the skies to kill mighty Air Wizard!
